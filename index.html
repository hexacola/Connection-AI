Manual Installation

  1. Download or clone the GitHub repository
  2. Open Chrome and navigate to chrome://extensions/
  3. Enable "Developer mode" by toggling the switch in the top-right corner
  4. Click "Load unpacked" and select the directory containing the extension
  5. The Connection AI extension is now installed and ready to use

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Connection AI

Is Connection AI free to use?

+

Yes, Connection AI is completely free to use, and now includes free access to all premium features! Simply activate the free premium subscription within the extension to enjoy unlimited personalities, premium styles, and enhanced features.

Which AI models power Connection AI?

+

Connection AI uses the Pollinations.AI API, which provides access to advanced AI models from OpenAI, Mistral, and others, selected automatically based on your needs.

Does Connection AI work offline?

+

No, Connection AI requires an internet connection to communicate with its AI backend services for generating responses and fixing grammar.

Can I use Connection AI on any website?

+

Yes, Connection AI works on most websites where text can be selected. Some secure websites may restrict content scripts, but the extension is designed to work across the web.

How do I sign in to Connection AI?

+

Click the Connection AI icon in your browser toolbar to open the popup. Then click "Sign in with Google" to authenticate using your Google account.

What are AI personalities and how do I use them?

+

AI personalities allow you to create custom AI assistants with specific traits, communication styles, and knowledge areas. To create a personality, open the extension popup, go to the Personality tab, and click "Add New Personality". You can then customize various aspects including traits, communication style, and response length.

How do I activate the free premium subscription?

+

To activate the free premium subscription, open the extension popup, navigate to the Subscription tab, and click "Subscribe Now". The system will automatically activate the free premium subscription without requiring any payment information.
